Privacy Policy

Flood & Landslide Early-Warning Advisory Chatbot
Last updated: April 2026  ·  MPhil Research Project, University of Moratuwa

This chatbot is an academic research prototype that provides disaster safety information and collects voluntary community hazard reports via WhatsApp. This page explains what data we collect, how it is used, and how you can opt out.

1. What Data We Collect

DataHow it is storedPurpose
Phone number One-way SHA-256 hash (first 16 hex chars). The original number is never written to any database. Anonymous user identity for reliability scoring; duplicate detection
District & language preference Stored in registrations.db against a pseudonymous ID Sending district-specific early-warning alerts in your language
Hazard report content Stored in community_reports.db with phone hash only (no name, no number) Community situational awareness; research on VGI credibility scoring
Conversation messages Held in temporary session memory (in-process, not persisted to disk) Maintaining conversation context within a single session
Tool-call logs Stored with phone hash; no message content, only which tool was called Research evaluation dataset (routing accuracy)

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name (unless you voluntarily provide it during registration)
  • Your location or GPS coordinates
  • Any data from your device beyond the WhatsApp message text
  • Payment or financial information

3. How Data is Used

Data is used solely for the purposes of this MPhil research project:

  • Delivering disaster safety information and district-level alerts via WhatsApp
  • Aggregating community hazard reports to improve situational awareness
  • Evaluating the performance of the AI agent (research dataset)

Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. Aggregated, anonymised findings may be published in academic papers.

4. Data Retention

Community hazard reports are automatically archived or deleted after 7–180 days depending on hazard type. Registration records are kept for the duration of the study and deleted upon study completion.

5. How to Opt Out

Send STOP to the chatbot at any time via WhatsApp. This immediately removes you from the alert distribution list and suppresses your data from all future processing. No further messages will be sent to you.

6. Third-Party Services

This chatbot uses the following external services. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • WhatsApp / Meta — message delivery
  • OpenAI — AI language model (message text is processed by GPT-4o-mini)
  • Open-Meteo — rainfall data (district coordinates only, no personal data)

7. Contact

This is an academic research project conducted at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. For questions or data deletion requests please contact the researcher via the university.
